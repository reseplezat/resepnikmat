Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Cah Ayam Brokoli ||Broccoli Chicken:

  1. 200 gr fillet Dada ayam/200 gr chicken breast filet
  2. 100 gr Brokoli/100 gr broccoli
  3. 1 buah bawang Bombay, potong-potong/1 onion, chopped
  4. 3 siung bawang putih, cincang/3 garlic, chopped
  5. 1 SDM kecap asin/1 tablespoon of salty Soy sauce
  6. Garam dan lada bubuk secukupnya/salt and pepper for taste
  7. secukupnya Minyak goreng
  8. 1 sdt maizena (cairkan dengan sedikit air)
  9. 1 teaspoon of corn starch, put a little bit of water and stir
  10. Kaldu jamur secukupnya/mushroom broth powder
  11. 1 sdt perasan jeruk nipis/1 teaspoon of lime juice
  12. Kaldu sayur (saya skip)/Vegetable broth,i skip
  13. 1 SDM saus tiram/1 tablespoon of oyster Sauce

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Cah Ayam Brokoli ||Broccoli Chicken

1
Marinasi ayam dgn 1 sdm kecap asin, beri sedikit perasan jeruk nipis. PotongÂ² brokoli. Didihkan air, tambah sedikit garam. Rebus brokoli sebentar.Angkat dan langsung masukkan ke air es, agar warna brokoli tetap hijau segar. Marinate the chicken with salty Soy sauce and give a little bit of lime water. Cut the broccolis,boiled the water, add some salt.Cook the broccoli a while in the water and take out from the water then put in the ice water so that the color of broccoli still fresh green.
2
Setelah brokoli dingin, angkat dan tiriskan.Tumis bawang putih, lalu masukkan bawang bombay, tumis hingga harum. Masukkan ayam yg sudah dimarinasi. After the broccoli cool down,then take out from the water and filtered. Stir fry the garlic then add the onion. Stir well until the aroma comes out. Put inside the marinated chicken.
3
Masukkan brokoli, tambahkan gula, garam, kaldu jamur, kaldu sayur secukupnya, saus tiram dan lada. Terakhir tambahkan larutan maizena. Add some broccoli,put the sugar, salt and mushroom broth powder, vegetable broth,oyster Sauce. Then add some of the maizena mixture.
4
Angkat dari api dan sajikan cah Ayam Brokoli dengan nasi hangat. Take out from the heat. And serve broccoli chicken with the warm rice.
