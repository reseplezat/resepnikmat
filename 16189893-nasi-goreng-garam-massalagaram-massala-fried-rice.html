Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Nasi Goreng Garam Massala||Garam Massala Fried Rice:

  1. 1 piring Nasi putih/1 plate of cooked white rice
  2. 1 butir telur,skip/1 egg,skip
  3. 2 buah Sosi sapi,iris serong/2 beaf sausage, sliced diagonally
  4. Kerupuk goreng secukupnya/chips fried
  5. optional Daging sapi kornet, tambahan dari saya/Beaf Cornet
  6. 1 sdt Garam Masala/1 teaspoon of garam massala
  7. 1 buah paprika merah, potong dadu/1 red pepper, diced
  8. 1 buah sosis, goreng/1 sausage, fried
  9. secukupnya Garam dan kaldu bubuk/salt and Meat powder
  10. 1 SDM Kol,iris - iris(saya paprika hijau, potong dadu)
  11. 1 tablespoon of sliced cabbage, (me green pepper,diced)

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Nasi Goreng Garam Massala||Garam Massala Fried Rice

1
Panaskan minyak atau margarin,lalu tumis paprika merah, paprica hijau, sosis dan kol hingga matang. Masukkan kornet sapi,aduk rata hingga matang dan harum. Siapkan kerupuk goreng. Heat up the oil or margarine or butter,then stir fry the red paprica,green paprica, cabbage and sausage until cooked well. Then add the beaf Cornet,mix well until it cooked and the aroma comes out. Prepare the fried chips.
2
Tambahkan garam,kaldu sapi bubuk, aduk rata. Tambahkan garam massala,sosis goreng. Aduk rata. Put the beaf powder, salt, into the mixture. Stir well. Then add the garam masala,fried sausage. Stir well.
3
Tuang nasi ke dalam piring saji. Tata kerupuk. Sajikan hangat. Served the rice in the plate. Put some chips. Served warmth.
